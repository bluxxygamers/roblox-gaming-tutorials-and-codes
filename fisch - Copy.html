🦈 Welcome to Fisch 🦈

Dive into Fisch, the ultimate fishing adventure where exploration and progression are yours to control. Cast your line to catch rare and unique fish, each with 400,000+ possible variations. Explore a growing world with your trusty rod, and maybe even reel in some new friends along the way!

[How To Fish]
• Hold down to cast your bobber
• Await a bite. You can "shake" your rod to attract fish faster
• When you have a bite, hold down or click to pull the white bar. Follow the blue line to the best of your ability; it's okay to mess up a few times!
• When the progress bar on the bottom is full, you'll reel in your catch!
